Data Library on Industry Closures and Reopening

          This page provides access to and explains the construction of the datasets
          used in "Reopening the Economy: What Are the Risks, and What Have
          States Done?" These datasets were constructed from text of states' and
          municipalities' executive orders, promulgated between March and July, 2020.

Excel Files, Underlying Initial Industry Designations

    Here, we provide the excel files helpful in characterizing industries' initial
    essential designations. The "Keyword-Based Designations" file presents our
    definitions from a group ofstates with extensive keyword-based lists. The
    "Designations in Other States" file presents our definitions for a second group
    of states, which we have hand-classified.
