Data Library on Industry Closures and Reopening

          This page provides access to and explains the construction of the datasets
          used in "Reopening the Economy: What Are the Risks, and What Have
          States Done?" These datasets were constructed from text of states' and
          municipalities' executive orders, promulgated between March 2020 and April
          2021.

Excel Files, Underlying Reopening

    Here, we provide the excel files helpful in characterizing the reopening process.
    The first seven files pertain to states with phased in region-by-region reopening
    plans in the spring and summer of 2020. The penultimate file charactrizes
    reopening in all other states. The final file collects California counties' tier status,
    beginning in September 2020.
